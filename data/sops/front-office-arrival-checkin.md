# Arrival and Check-In Procedure

**Property:** The Cannon & Vine, Nashville, TN
**Department:** Front Office
**Document Owner:** General Manager
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose

This procedure governs the arrival and check-in process for all guests at The Cannon & Vine. The standard ensures a consistent, efficient, and personalized first impression while accurately capturing guest information and payment authorization in Cloudbeds.

## Scope

Applies to all Front Desk Agents, Night Auditors handling after-hours check-ins, and the Manager on Duty. Covers pre-registered arrivals, early arrivals, and after-hours arrivals. Walk-in reservations are handled under a separate SOP.

## Responsibilities

- **Front Desk Agent:** Performs all steps in the check-in sequence; flags discrepancies to the Manager; completes folio authorization at check-in.
- **Night Auditor:** Performs check-in procedures between 11:00 PM and 7:00 AM using the same steps; escalates unresolved issues to the Manager on Duty by cell phone.
- **Manager / General Manager:** Approves rate adjustments or comp decisions exceeding $50; approves folio authorization holds below the standard amount.

## Pre-Shift Preparation

At shift start, the Front Desk Agent completes the following before any guest arrivals:

1. Log into Cloudbeds and open the Reservations Dashboard, filtered to today's arrivals.
2. Review each expected arrival in the Guest Card for return guest status, special requests, celebration notes, and accessibility requirements.
3. Verify pre-assigned rooms in the Room Rack are showing "Clean and Inspected." Coordinate with the Director of Housekeeping on any rooms still showing "Dirty" or "In Progress" on reservations expected before 3:00 PM.
4. Pull up the shift Arrivals Report for quick reference during peak hours.

## Standard Check-In Sequence

All guests arriving between 3:00 PM and 11:00 PM follow this sequence.

1. Greet the guest within 10 seconds of their approach to the desk. Use the guest's name once confirmed: "Welcome to The Cannon & Vine, Ms. Reyes."
2. Pull up the Guest Card in Cloudbeds by last name or confirmation number.
3. Confirm reservation details aloud: arrival date, departure date, room type. Do not read the rate — confirm it silently and ask, "and we have everything confirmed on your reservation?"
4. Request a government-issued photo ID. Scan per property policy and attach to the Guest Card.
5. Capture payment authorization. Run an incidental hold of $75 per night plus the room and tax total through the Billing tab in Cloudbeds.
6. If the hold fails, pause the check-in silently. Offer an alternate card or payment method. Do not announce the failure in front of other guests.
7. Acknowledge at least one personalization note for return guests: "I see you had the king room facing Broadway last time — we've got you in 412 this trip."
8. Confirm final room assignment. Verify room status is "Clean and Inspected" before proceeding.
9. Program keys and hand them to the guest in a key packet with the WiFi password and restaurant hours noted.
10. Walk the guest through a brief orientation: elevator location, restaurant hours, breakfast location.
11. Close with a return-to-desk invitation: "If anything comes up, we're here 24/7 — just dial zero."

## Early Arrivals (Before 3:00 PM)

If the guest arrives before 3:00 PM and their room is not yet ready:

1. Welcome the guest and confirm reservation details per steps 1–4 above.
2. Toggle to the Housekeeping view in Cloudbeds. Look for any room of the reserved type showing "Clean and Inspected."
3. If a matching clean room is available, reassign the room and continue standard check-in.
4. If no clean room is available, offer luggage storage at the front desk and provide a property map with restaurant and lobby bar notes.
5. Collect the guest's mobile number and record it in the Guest Card notes. Promise a text when the room is ready.
6. Text the guest via Kipsu the moment the room passes inspection.

## After-Hours Arrivals (After 11:00 PM)

For arrivals after 11:00 PM, the Night Auditor follows the full check-in sequence with these modifications:

1. Verify guest identity through photo ID before discussing any reservation details.
2. Reference the Arrivals Report printed by the evening shift for expected late arrivals.
3. If the guest is not on the expected arrivals list, verify a valid reservation in Cloudbeds before proceeding.
4. Keep voices low in the lobby after midnight. Deliver orientation on a pre-printed card rather than spoken.

## Escalation Triggers

Contact the Manager on Duty immediately if:
- Payment authorization fails on two cards
- Guest disputes the rate or room assignment
- Photo ID cannot be verified or is expired
- Room is not ready by 4:30 PM and no alternate is available
- Guest is uncooperative or appears impaired
